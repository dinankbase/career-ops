# Creative Audit Template

Use this template to build a Creative Audit for any company before an interview. The audit demonstrates you understand the PROBLEM the company solves and can think strategically about their creative.

**Adapted from the BowTiedCocoon 5-P Matrix for creative strategy roles.**

**When to use:**
- Round 2-3 with Hiring Manager or Creative Director
- Any round with multiple interviewers (the "wow" effect multiplies in groups)
- After you've already built some positive bias (don't lead with this in a recruiter screen)

**How to present:**
1. Email 2 hours before the call: "I put together some thoughts on your creative. Mind if we take 5 min?"
2. Or during the interview: "I spent some time in your Ad Library. I have some observations if you're interested."

## How to Present This in 5 Minutes

1. **30 sec:** "I looked at your ads in Meta Ad Library. Here's what I noticed." (Section E summary)
2. **60 sec:** "Here's what I think is working well." (Acknowledge strengths first)
3. **60 sec:** "Here's what I think is missing." (Section F, 2-3 gaps)
4. **90 sec:** "Here's what I'd test." (Section G, your top 2 concepts)
5. **30 sec:** "I'd love to hear your thoughts. How does this match what you're seeing internally?"

**Rules:**
- Frame as "observations and hypotheses," not "here's what you're doing wrong"
- Acknowledge what's working before suggesting improvements
- Ask for their reaction - make it a conversation, not a presentation
- If they push back on something, be coachable: "That's a good point. How would you approach it?"

## Building This Audit in 15-30 Minutes

1. **Meta Ad Library** (5 min): Search the brand, screenshot 5-10 ads, note formats and hooks
2. **Company website** (5 min): Check testimonials, case studies, product pages for customer language
3. **Review sites** (5 min): G2/Trustpilot/Amazon/Reddit for problem statements in customer words
4. **Competitor ads** (5 min): Quick check of 2-3 competitors to see what angles are common
5. **Draft concepts** (10 min): Based on gaps, draft 2-3 spec concepts with hooks

For high-priority targets, add:
6. **Sign up for their email list** (to see their email sequences and funnel)
7. **Book a demo** (if B2B SaaS - observe their sales pitch and discovery questions)
8. **Deep customer research** (Reddit threads, YouTube comments, Amazon reviews for real language)
